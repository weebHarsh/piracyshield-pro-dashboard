# Required Fonts — Pangram Pangram (Free)

Download from https://pangrampangram.com/products/

Place the `.woff2` files at these exact paths:

## PP Editorial New
- `pp-editorial-new/PPEditorialNew-Ultralight.woff2`
- `pp-editorial-new/PPEditorialNew-UltralightItalic.woff2`
- `pp-editorial-new/PPEditorialNew-Light.woff2`

## PP Neue Montreal
- `pp-neue-montreal/PPNeueMontreal-Book.woff2`
- `pp-neue-montreal/PPNeueMontreal-Medium.woff2`
- `pp-neue-montreal/PPNeueMontreal-Bold.woff2`

## PP Fraktion Mono
- `pp-fraktion-mono/PPFraktionMono-Regular.woff2`
- `pp-fraktion-mono/PPFraktionMono-Medium.woff2`

Until the files are placed here, the app falls back to system-ui (sans) and
ui-monospace (mono) — fully functional but without the typographic character.
